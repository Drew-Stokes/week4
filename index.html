Cruises

The Eastern Travel Express Line offers a series of ships built specifically to travel the rivers of Southeast Asia in a style that is consistent with colonial times and traditions. The ships are small, having three decks and accommodating 65 passengers in 200 sq. ft. staterooms.
Staterooms are equiped with A/C, private bathrooms with shower and hair dryers.

Destinations

The Eastern Travel Hotel Group offers high-quality luxury hotel spaces in many East Asian destinations of interest. From Hong Kong Bay to the Con Dao Island Archipelago, you will find the highest quality and the most amenities when you book with us.
All rooms are exquisitely tailored to the most discerning demands of the high-class traveler.
